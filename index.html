Handcrafted Heaven

An original poem

by Alyssa Vogel


I'm envious of the birds high up in the trees.

Because birds live out their lives so consciously.

I envy the spiders.

The squirrels.

The seeds.

They all find their way undoubtedly.


But in this handcrafted heaven, I come alive.

Beautiful blue birds and butterflies will forever paint the sky.

In this handcrafted heaven, we forget the time.

Because birds of a feather fly together.


Now that I can finally see the flowers in the greenery.

I take a breath of air.

I finally feel free.
